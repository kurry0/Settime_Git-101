Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are one of my favourite foods. Here is a major recipe for deliciously made scrambled eggs

Items Needed

  1. Cooking
    • Eggs
    • Maggi
    • Salt
    • Butter
  2. Kitchen Utensils
    • Big spoon
    • Small pot
    • Pot
    • Bas/Stove

Method

  1. Melt the butter in a frying pan over a medium heat.
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl.
  3. Once butter has melted add cream and eggs.
  4. Using a spatula.
  5. When the eggs are still moist remove from the heat.
